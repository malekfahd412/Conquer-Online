Task 10 — Ticket System Pro (Phase 3)

Objective

Build a complete Permission Designer for Ticket Panels.

This is NOT just selecting support roles.

This is a full permission management interface similar to Ticket Tool, but more powerful.

Everything must be configurable from the Ticket Panel Designer.

No JSON editing.

No slash commands.

Everything from Discord.
